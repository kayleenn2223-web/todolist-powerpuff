TROUBLESHOOTING & SUPPORT
==============================================================================

COMMON ISSUES & SOLUTIONS:

Issue: "404 Not Found"
Solution: Check folder path, proper URL, Apache running

Issue: "Data folder not found"
Solution: App creates it auto, or create manually with 755 permissions

Issue: "Email already registered"
Solution: Email must be unique, use different email

Issue: "Passwords don't match"
Solution: Check they're exactly same, case-sensitive, no extra spaces

Issue: "Tasks not saving"
Solution: Refresh page, check permissions, verify data folder writable

Issue: "Can't login after logout"
Solution: Clear browser cache, delete cookies, restart browser
